ABOUT US
Little Fingers India Pvt. Ltd. is a leading manufacturer of indoor and outdoor children's play
equipment, fitness equipment, and toys.
Founded in 2006 as importers and traders, the company expanded into manufacturing in
2016, marking a significant milestone in our growth journey. With a strong commitment to
quality, safety, and innovation, we design and manufacture products that promote physical
activity, creativity, and holistic development for children.
Today, Little Fingers India Pvt. Ltd. has a Pan-India presence and also caters to international
markets through exports. Our products are widely used in schools, parks, playgrounds,
residential communities, fitness spaces, and institutional environments.
Driven by customer trust and industry experience, we continue to deliver durable, safe, and
engaging play and fitness solutions that meet national and international standards.
